What is EcoRise?

EcoRise is an educational sustainable agriculture simulator that uses real NASA data to help farmers and students make better crop decisions.

Learn about your land conditions, local climate, and get personalized recommendations from Bobby, your sustainable agriculture guide, to maximize your crop effectiveness responsibly with the environment.

Our Mission

Agriculture faces unprecedented challenges: climate change, population growth, and the need for more sustainable practices. Farmers need accessible tools that enable them to make informed decisions based on real scientific data.

EcoRise was born as a response to this challenge, combining NASA's open data with interactive technology to make scientific information accessible and practical. Through our simulator, farmers and students can experiment with different crop scenarios without risk, learning the best sustainable agriculture practices.

With Bobby as a friendly guide, we transform complex data on temperature, soil, climate, and rain probability into clear and actionable recommendations. Each decision in EcoRise helps you understand the real impact that sustainable practices have on your crop effectiveness.

How Does EcoRise Work?

A simple process that guides you step by step towards more sustainable and informed agricultural decisions

1. Select Your Location

Use your current location or select any point on the map. EcoRise will obtain specific climate and soil data from NASA for that area.

2. Configure Your Crop

Define your land dimensions and choose the type of plant you want to grow: tomato, corn, or lettuce. Each crop has its own needs.

3. Get Predictions

Visualize your crop's expected effectiveness based on soil temperature, soil type, product demand, and rain probability. Bobby will give you personalized advice.
